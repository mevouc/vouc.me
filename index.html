Bentley Systems

I am currently holding a Software Engineer position at Bentley Systems, in Paris office.

During my first 2 years at Bentley, as a part of the on-boarding program, I first was switching teams within Bentley every 6 months. I worked in turns on reality modeling projects, 3D rendering softwares and user interfaces, in C++, C# and TypeScript.

I am now a permanent developer, part a the Visualization team. My daily work consists of maintaining and improving E-on software products, especially VUE, a toolset for creating CG environments.
